Process

How It Works

Simple, fast, and reliable — mediQ fits right into your existing workflow.

01

Register Medicine in Database

Add each medicine with its complete information — name, batch number, manufacturer, manufacture date, expiry date, quantity, price, and storage location. A unique mediQ ID (e.g. MQ-0001) is auto-generated.

02

Generate & Attach QR Code

Each medicine entry can have a QR code generated and printed, encoding the medicine's ID and key data. Attach it to the packaging or shelf label.

03

Scan to Retrieve Instantly

Point the mediQ QR scanner at any medicine label. The system decodes the QR, looks up the database, and displays full medicine details in under a second.

04

Monitor & Get Alerts

The dashboard continuously monitors stock levels and expiry dates. You receive instant visual alerts for low stock items and medicines nearing their expiry date.
